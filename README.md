## What is Project Baccara?

Takanock LLC (Michigan-based) is proposing to build a massive data center complex with 18 natural gas jet turbine generators just half a mile from Surprise, AZ city limits. The project includes:

- **700 MW** of natural gas power generation
- **18 jet turbine generators** with 72-foot exhaust stacks
- **~59 million gallons** of water usage annually
- Located in **one of the most air-polluted counties in America**
